What we’re building and why we’re building it. 

Fetch is a build-first technology company creating a rewards program to power the world. Over the last 5 years, we’ve grown from 0 to 17M active users and taken over the rewards game in the US with our free app. The foundation has been laid. In the next 5 years we will become a global platform that completely transforms how people connect with brands. 

It all comes down to two core beliefs. First, that people deserve to be rewarded when they create value. If a third party directly benefits from an action you take or data you provide, you should be rewarded for it. And not just the “you get to use our product!” cop-out. We’re talkin’ real, explicit value. Fetch points, perhaps. 

Second, we also believe brands need a better and more direct connection with what matters most to them: their customers. -- Brands need to understand what people are doing, and have a direct line to be able to do something about it. Not just advertise, but ACT. Sounds nice right? 

That’s why we’re building the world’s rewards platform. A closed-loop, standardized rewards layer across all consumer behavior that will lead to happier shoppers and stronger brands.

The Role: 

The Catalog team at Fetch is being built from the ground up, and you will have the opportunity to make a huge impact on this team & the future of product data here at Fetch!  Are you fascinated by why consumers buy the products they buy? How do all these products relate to or influence one another? Would you LOVE the chance to play in the massive dataset that is Fetch's world of scanned products? That's this team!

The catalog team has an ambitious goal: to build a global catalog of all of the products that can be bought - and that make their way into the millions of receipts that are scanned daily in our app. In addition to curating this ambitious product catalog alongside your team, you’ll also be developing a taxonomy to “make sense” of this catalog, ultimately driving better insight into what users shop. You’ll be working closely with other Fetch teams to understand how this entire product catalog is consumed, plus working towards improving the scope, fidelity & accuracy of its use.

You Possess: 

  • Superlative communication skills & the ability to process complex ideas simply & concisely
  • Solid SQL skills (primarily SELECTs) or exemplary Excel skills (be prepared to “wow!”)
  • Interest in building and experimenting with different tools and tech, and sharing your learnings with the broader organization
  • A strong desire to work with other teams in the organization (e.g., Development, Business Intelligence, Data Science, Engineering) to build tools and data solutions that support the wider Fetch ecosystem
  • 2+ years’ relevant full-time work experience (or relevant post-grad degree)

Bonus points for: 

  • A background in library science, analytics, database management or computer science
  • Experience programming in Python, Java, or R
  • Experience working with large,multi-source e-commerce datasets or consumer packaged goods (CPG) reporting
  • Familiarity with Unix systems, shell scripting, and Git
  • Prior experience working with engineering teams driving insights or delivery business requirements that shape development of relevant tech
  • Expertise in proficiencies like Regular Expressions, entity resolution, fuzzy matching, data-quality metrics & measurement, UPC standards, product attribution, item categorization, and taxonomy creation & refinement
  • Experience with visualization tools (e.g., Tableau)
  • Love of Dogs! . . . Or just tolerance. We're a very canine-friendly workplace
